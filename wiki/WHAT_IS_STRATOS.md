# What is Stratos? (In Simple Terms)

Stratos is a suite of three powerful software tools designed to help modern businesses operate smarter, automate their daily work, and get more value out of their data.

Instead of buying three completely different software packages from different companies, Stratos provides three interconnected "mini-products" that all talk to each other under one roof.

Here is what each of the three products does in plain English:

---

## 1. Aurum (The "Crystal Ball" for Revenue)
**What it does:** It helps companies keep their customers happy and predicts how much money they will make.

**How it works:**
Imagine you run a subscription software business. Aurum looks at how often your customers log in, what buttons they click, and how many issues they report. 
- **Health Scores:** It gives every customer a "Health Score" (like a credit score) so you instantly know who is happy and who is frustrated.
- **Churn Prediction:** Using Artificial Intelligence, it flags customers who act like they are going to cancel their subscription *before* they actually quit, so your team can reach out and save them.
- **Revenue Forecasting:** It predicts exactly how much money your business will make next month based on current trends.

---

## 2. Meridian (The "Auto-Pilot" for Workflows)
**What it does:** It lets you build automated, behind-the-scenes processes without needing to write code every time.

**How it works:**
Every company has repetitive tasks. For example, "When a new customer signs up, send them a welcome email, create a folder for them in Google Drive, and alert the sales team on Slack."
- Instead of paying a programmer to write custom script for this, Meridian gives you a visual Drag-and-Drop canvas.
- You can draw lines between different "action boxes" to build these workflows.
- What makes Meridian special is that it has **AI bots built straight into the flow**. So you can create a workflow that says: "When we get a customer support email, have ChatGPT read it, summarize it, and then email the team."

---

## 3. Vektor (The "Smart Search Engine" for Private Data)
**What it does:** It acts like a private ChatGPT that only knows about your specific company's documents.

**How it works:**
Standard AI models like ChatGPT are great, but they don't know anything about *your* private company handbooks, private customer guides, or secret internal PDFs.
- Vektor allows you to safely upload all your company's private documents into a secure database.
- It slices those vast documents up into small, readable chunks and "memorizes" their meaning.
- Then, your employees can use Vektor just like Google or ChatGPT: "What is our company policy on remote work for 2024?" Vektor instantly searches through thousands of your private PDFs, finds the exact paragraph, and uses an AI to type out a helpful, conversational answer based *only* on your actual documents.

---

### In Summary
- **Aurum** protects your revenue.
- **Meridian** automates your chores.
- **Vektor** organizes your knowledge.
